## Storage Areas
<br>

Before addressing the actual processing, it is essential to plan for adequate storage space.
{.text-lg .mb-4}

<div class="flex flex-col md:flex-row md:items-start space-y-4 md:space-y-0 md:space-x-6">
 <div class="w-full md:w-1/2">
    <br>
    <p class="text-lg mb-4">Whether for fresh produce in a cellar or for preserves and dried goods in a pantry, there are often overlooked considerations that must be taken into account:</p>
    <br>
        <ol class="text-lg mb-4 pl-6">
          <li>the volume of storage space in these rooms, which must correspond to the volume of the harvest,</li>
          <li>practical organization using shelves and storage units adapted to the weight,</li>
          <li>the humidity level and temperature of the room, specific to the products that will be stored there,</li>
          <li>easy access for checking the condition of the food and jars,</li>
          <li>adequate ventilation, especially for fresh produce,</li>
          <li>regular checks for the absence of rodents and insects.</li>
        </ol>
    <br>
    <p class="text-lg mb-4">It is useful to build custom storage shelves to save space, based on standard commercial sizes for cardboard boxes and crates that can be easily collected and replaced free of charge.</p>
    <br>

        <ol class="text-lg mb-4 pl-6">

## Transformations into Spices & Herbal Tea Blends

<br>

After the drying/dehydration stage, and depending on the crops, we offer numerous varieties of aromatic herbs, spices, and condiments.
{.text-lg .mb-4}

Regarding roots, such as turmeric and ginger, which are quite easy to grow in planters, we dry them with a dehydrator or near a heat source and grind them to make extraordinary spices.
{.text-lg .mb-4}

We do the same with certain varieties of bell peppers and chili peppers, which are dried and ground to obtain succulent condiments like paprika.
{.text-lg .mb-4}

Finally, we make homemade herbal teas by keeping the dried leaves to enjoy their benefits all winter long.
As an added bonus, if we dry, for example, violet or mallow flowers, rose petals, etc., we can create blends with verbena or lemongrass from our garden. The possibilities are endless.
{.text-lg .mb-4}
