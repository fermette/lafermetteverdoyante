We will offer various workshops throughout the year focusing on the skills we develop on-site.

In order to preserve biodiversity and the tranquility of the environment, <b>workshops will be limited to a maximum of 7 people or fewer</b>, depending on the resources needed for effective implementation and knowledge transfer.

A fee will be charged based on the nature of the event, the costs associated with its organization, and the number of instructors present. The average duration will be half a day (approximately 3 hours, with possible overtime) for an approximate price of €40 per person. Depending on availability, several half-day or full-day sessions with a shared meal on-site can be organized.

For participants traveling from afar, addresses of guesthouses, bed and breakfasts, or WWOOFing opportunities can be shared. A schedule of several half-day training sessions can also be proposed to combine different workshops.

The workshops are an essential source of income for the association's operation and future development. We express our gratitude to all current and future participants.
